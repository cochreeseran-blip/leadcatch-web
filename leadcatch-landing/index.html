About

Built by someone who's
been in the field.

Reese Cochran
Founder & CEO

LeadCatch Solutions was built on a simple observation: most businesses have the right people, but the wrong systems. Leads fall through cracks. Reps work without accountability. Managers make decisions without visibility. The result is wasted effort and lost revenue that never shows up on a report.

Before founding LeadCatch, our team worked directly in door-to-door roofing and car sales — learning firsthand where the breakdowns happen between field activity and closed business. That experience drives everything we build.

We don't consult from a boardroom. We build real systems for real teams — software you can actually deploy, processes your people will actually follow, and tools that tie effort directly to revenue.

If your business is running on spreadsheets, gut instinct, and group texts — we can do better than that. Let's build something that works.
